Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

Glean Photos ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

Glean Photos is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

  • Photo Library Access: The App requests access to your device's photo library solely to display, organize, and delete photos and videos at your direction. All photo data remains on your device.
  • Organization Tags: Your keep/delete decisions are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted externally.
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

2. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers or cloud services. If you delete the App, all locally stored data will be removed.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple system services only:

  • Apple Photos Framework: To access and manage your photo library with your permission.
  • Apple StoreKit: To process in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled by Apple under their Privacy Policy.

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any of your data to third parties. Your photos and data never leave your device.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. No account creation or personal information input is required.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at: gleanphotos@outlook.com

お客様のデータを第三者と共有、販売、譲渡することはありません。写真やデータがデバイスから外部に送信されることはありません。

当アプリは、13歳未満のお子様から意図的に情報を収集することはありません。アカウントの作成や個人情報の入力は不要です。
